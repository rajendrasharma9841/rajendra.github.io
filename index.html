ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Rajendra Sharma and I am an self trained interior designer based in Nepal. With 24 years of experience in the industry, I specialize in creating beautiful and functional spaces that reflect my clients' unique tastes and lifestyles.
Whether it's selecting the perfect color palette, choosing the right furniture and accessories, or optimizing the layout of a space, I am committed to delivering exceptional results that exceed my clients' expectations.

Thank you for considering me for your interior design needs. I look forward to the opportunity to work with you!
